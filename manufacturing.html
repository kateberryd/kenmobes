About Manufacturing

Ken Mobes Investment Company Limited has been in the business of manufacturing high-quality nylons and various nylon products in Nigeria. Our subsidiary manufacturing plant which is registered as Destiny Plastics Industry is stocked with first-class state of the art machineries that are imported from various parts of the world. We are the leading manufacturer of engineered nylon, and with one of the broadest product portfolios in the industry. Ken Mobes Investment Company Limited has established itself as a leading supplier of high-performance nylons in Nigeria.

We make the chemicals, polymers, fabrics, and fibers that can handle life’s toughest demands. Experts at Ken Mobes Investment Company are committed to create new solutions for life’s biggest needs and deliver products that can be incorporated into your clothing, your car, and even your carpet.We can also custom cut any Nylon products to your individual specifications.

At Ken Mobes Investment Company Limited, we highly focus on innovation continuously puts creativity and technology to work, and finding new ways to make our products better. We only use the best available industrial technologies under strict supervision of a professional team of industry specialists and quality control experts. This effort is supported by a well-equipped lab ensuring strict adherence to local and international quality.

We constantly seek to offer high returns to our stakeholders by focusing on three main goals: exceeding consumer expectations, providing premier quality product, and continuously investing in and developing the company’s human resource capabilities and technical capacities.
