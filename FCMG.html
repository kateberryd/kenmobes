About FCMG(Fast Moving Consumer Goods)

Ken Mobes Investment Company Limited has been in the business of FMCG (Fast Moving Consumer Goods) distribution for over 24 years. We are authorized distributors of very popular brands such as Nigerian Breweries Plc, Guinness Nigeria Plc and International Breweries and have supplying their products to different locations and different sectors within Nigeria. As we’re one of the leading distributors of FMCG brands, we have deep understanding and expertise of the FMCG market, acquired through years of experience. This allows us to expertly serve and support our customers with their various business requirements, and help them successfully entrench their business within the FMCG market.

At Ken Mobes Investment Company Limited, we have well experienced and talented sales team that always provide timely delivery to our customers and assist them with their enquiries. Our warehouse staff are also very experienced in order picking and stock consolidation providing efficient and speedy delivery. We always welcome new brands to work with us so we can provide them high-quality distribution service and ensure that their product’s quality is never compromised.
